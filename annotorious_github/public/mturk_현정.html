Definitions of Voice Features

Pitch : The degree of highness or lowness of a tone.

Loudness : The degree of loudness in volumn.

Hoarseness : The degree of roughness and harshness of a voice, typically as the result of a sore throat or of shouting

Speed : The speech rate.

Variation : The rise and fall of the voice in speaking; Monotonous means lacking in variation in tone or pitch.

Smoothness : The level of smoothness of the sound.

Breathiness : The rate of the usage of air-flow; breathy sound means high air-flow.

Preference : The level of preference. Strong preference means you like the voice very much.
